॥ gurustotram ॥

aTa gurustotram ।

bfhaspatiH surAcAryo dayAvAn SuBalakzaRaH ।
lokatrayaguruH SrImAnsarvajYaH sarvakovidaH ॥ 1॥

sarveSaH sarvadA'BIzwaH sarvajitsarvapUjitaH ।
akroDano muniSrezWo nItikartA guruH pitA ॥ 2॥

viSvAtmA viSvakartA ca viSvayonirayonijaH ।
BUrBuvaHsuvaroM cEva BartA cEva mahAbalaH ॥ 3॥

paYcaviMSatinAmAni puRyAni niyatAtmanA ।
vasatA nandaBavane vizRunA kIrtitAni vE ॥ 4॥

yaH paWet prAtarutTAya prayataH susamAhitaH ।
viparIto'pi BagavAnprIto Bavati vE guruH ॥ 5॥

yaSCfRoti gurustotraM ciraM jIvenna saMSayaH ।
bfhaspatikftA pIqA na kadAcidBavizyati ॥ 6॥

 iti gurustotraM sampUrRam ।
